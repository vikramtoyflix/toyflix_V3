# ToyJoy / Toyflix — Landing Page Brief for Emergent

## Product Overview
**ToyJoy** (branded as Toyflix in some contexts) is India's premier toy subscription service. Parents rent premium, educational toys for their children—curated boxes delivered monthly, no purchase needed, safe and sanitized.

**Target:** Indian parents with young children (roughly 0–6 years) who want quality toys without clutter or high cost.

---

## Current Homepage Structure (Top to Bottom)

1. **Hero Carousel** — Rotating slides with CTAs (Start journey, Explore toys, How it works)
2. **Banner Hero** — Tagline section (see below)
3. **Developmental Images Carousel** — 4 auto-scrolling images (see below)
4. **Rent Premium Toys** — Featured toy carousel
5. **Ride-On Toys** — Ride-on toys section
6. **Testimonials**
7. **Why Choose Us** / How it works
8. **Features Section**

## Key Messaging & Copy

### Primary Tagline (Banner Hero)
```
Play that matches your
child's growing mind.
```
- Two-line layout with a subtle divider line between them
- Reference style: Light blue gradient background, navy blue text, clean and ethereal

### Developmental Pillars (4 images with overlays)
1. **Motor Skills** — Grasping, Balance & coordination (baby with bead maze)
2. **Creativity & Confidence** — Pretend Play & Self-Expression (child with magnetic tiles)
3. **Problem-Solving** — Sorting, Puzzles & Early Logic (child with interlocking blocks)
4. **Language & Communication** — Babbling, First Words & Listening (baby with toy phone)

### Supporting Copy
- "Rent premium toys" — Curated collection, no purchase needed, endless fun
- "India's #1 Toy Subscription"

- "Premium, educational toys at your door every month. No clutter—just play and learning."
